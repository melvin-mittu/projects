Jeep Wrangler
The Jeep Wrangler is a five seater SUV car in the price range of Rs. 60.65 Lakh to 64.65 Lakh in Indian Market. It is offered in two variants and four colours. Jeep Wrangler is offered in BS6 compliant petrol engine only. Wrangler has ground clearance of 217 mm. The petrol engine of Wrangler is of 1998cc which generates power of 268bhp and torque of 400nm. The Wrangler is available only in automatic transmission option. The mileage of Wrangler is 12.1kmpl.
